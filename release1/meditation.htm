Did you know that "long-term, consistent meditation can increase resiliency to stress"?

According to a study by researchers, long term meditators:

  • had smaller cortisol and flare responses relative to non-meditators.
  • perceived the TSST as less stressful than non-meditators.
  • showed closer alignment between measured and perceived stress responses.
