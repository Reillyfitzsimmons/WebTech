Let's take a look at how you can incorperate traditional methods of meditation into your life.

This walkthrough from Tibetan Buddhist meditation master, Yongey Mingyur Rinpoche, will bring you through one of thousands of mindfulness practices that can start your journey to whatever goal you are trying to achieve. This is a very traditional type of minfulness and dates back to around the year of 988. One of the reasons in it still practiced today is because it can be so helpful. Mindfulness is not easy. It is a marathon that can take a long time to master, but for right now, all we are focused on is the present moment. And at this present moment, you have chosen the path of self-improvement. As Master Oogway once said "Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That is why it is called present."
