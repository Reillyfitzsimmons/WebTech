Hydration is the number one thing that humans can do to instantaniously boost mood, energy, and productivity.

Although it is not specific to hydration, Dr. Angela Duckworth provides detailed tactics that can help you start drinking more water daily. One tip she suggests is if you have a goal of drinking more water in the morning, put a full water bottle on your night stand the night before. This will allow you to wake up and not even have to think about drinking the water because it is right in front of you.

I have personally found this trick to be extremely helpful. I never used to drink water very much after I stopped playing sports, but after listening to this podcast, I tried it because it seemed really easy. I had no idea how much of a difference it would make in my mood every morning. Being hydrated allows your muscles to be more pliable. You want your muscles to be like a juicy serloin steak- not beef jerkey. Water and other hydrating beverages like orange juice can help you become that delicious piece of 12 oz. steak you know you can be.
