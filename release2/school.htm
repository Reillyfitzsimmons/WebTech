School is hard. Studying is hard. Life is hard. Here are some personal tips you can use to create better study habits:

  1. Create a schedule
  2. Start a study group
  3. Find your study spot
  4. Keep track of deadlines
  5. Spread it out

By doing these things, you can be sure that you are getting the most out of your learning experience. Afterall, the point of school is to learn.
